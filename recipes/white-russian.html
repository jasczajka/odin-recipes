White Russian

A White Russian is a cocktail made with vodka, coffee liqueur and cream served with ice in an old fashioned glass. Often milk, half and half, or cream liqueur will be used as an alternative to cream.

A rich and satisfying after-dinner drink blended from vodka, coffee liqueur, and light cream, the White Russian could almost be said to have it all. A one-stop source of alcohol, sugar, caffeine, and milk fat, the only legal substance it lacks is nicotine.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce Kahlúa
  • 1 splash heavy cream

Steps

  1. Add the vodka and Kahlúa to a rocks glass filled with ice.
  2. Top with the heavy cream and stir.
